Sovereign Intelligence Engine — Governance Notes

1. Purpose
The governance framework defines operational oversight, access control, and audit protocols for the Sovereign Intelligence Engine (SIE). It ensures that the engine remains under exclusive operational control, maintains integrity, and enforces compliance with defined ethical and security standards.

2. Access Control
- Only authorized personnel may initiate or query the engine.
- No external API or public access is allowed; all interactions must be mediated.
- Authentication is multi-factor and cryptographically enforced.
- Role-based permissions govern module access:
    * Administrator: Full control over all modules and configuration
    * Analyst: Read-only access to simulations, reports, and snapshots
    * Auditor: Read-only access with verification capabilities for reproducibility

3. Audit and Logging
- All module interactions are logged with timestamps, user IDs, and action signatures.
- Immutable snapshots capture system state at every major execution point.
- Versioned reports include metadata for traceability.
- Audit logs are stored in `/meta/audit_logs/` and are cryptographically hashed.

4. Decision-Making Protocols
- Autonomous actions of the engine require multi-signature approval from designated administrators.
- Any modification to core heuristics or axiomatic rules must be recorded with justification.
- Emergency intervention procedures exist to halt system execution in case of detected anomalies.

5. Compliance and Review
- Governance notes and operational procedures are reviewed quarterly.
- Independent auditors may verify logs and reports without executing the engine.
- All updates to governance protocols are versioned and documented in `/meta/versioning_and_changelog.txt`.

6. Ethical Constraints
- The engine must operate exclusively for research, strategic analysis, and modeling purposes.
- It must not be exposed for external public use or commercial exploitation.
- The system enforces safeguards to prevent unauthorized dissemination of sensitive outputs.

7. Enforcement
- Cryptographic signatures, audit chains, and multi-layer access control ensure compliance.
- Any deviation triggers automated alerts to the administrator group.
- Periodic audits verify adherence to all governance protocols.

These notes represent the living governance framework for the Sovereign Intelligence Engine, ensuring operational sovereignty, accountability, and ethical alignment.
