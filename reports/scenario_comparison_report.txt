# Sovereign Intelligence Engine: Scenario Comparison Report

## Overview
This report compares multiple scenario runs to identify cross-scenario trends, recurring micro-events, heuristic performance, and emergent patterns. It allows analysts to assess systemic stability, risk propagation, and effectiveness of adaptive heuristics.

## Report Fields

- scenario_ids: List of scenario identifiers being compared
- comparison_timestamp: Date and time of report generation
- summary_metrics:
    - total_micro_events
    - average_event_impact
    - heuristic_success_rate
    - cross_domain_propagation_rate
- domain_aggregates:
    - physics: aggregated stability metrics
    - biology: aggregated health and outbreak metrics
    - society: aggregated trust, compliance, and cohesion metrics
    - technology: aggregated integrity and disruption metrics
    - emergent: aggregated cascading or non-linear effects
- anomalies_detected: Notes on significant deviations or unexpected outcomes
- insights: Observed patterns across scenarios
- recommendations: Suggested follow-up scenarios or parameter adjustments

## Example Entry

scenario_ids: [SIE-2026-001, SIE-2026-002, SIE-2026-003]
comparison_timestamp: 2026-01-30T13:30:00Z

summary_metrics:
  total_micro_events: 128
  average_event_impact: moderate
  heuristic_success_rate: 92%
  cross_domain_propagation_rate: low

domain_aggregates:
  physics: stable
  biology: controlled_outbreaks
  society: trust_index_mean_68
  technology: network_integrity_mean_97
  emergent: minimal_cascading_effects

anomalies_detected:
  - Scenario SIE-2026-002 showed unexpected tech disruption from biology micro-event ME-045
  - Emergent effects in SIE-2026-003 propagated faster than predicted due to adaptive heuristic interactions

insights:
  - Adaptive heuristics consistently mitigated micro-event propagation
  - Biology and society domains remain the most sensitive to cross-domain micro-events
  - Emergent effects are predictable under high-resolution adaptive configurations

recommendations:
  - Adjust node priority weights to better protect sensitive domains
  - Conduct additional runs with alternative micro-event seeding strategies
  - Continue monitoring emergent layer interactions for anomalous behavior
