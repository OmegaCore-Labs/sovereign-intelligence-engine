# Sovereign Intelligence Engine: Scenario Comparison Report vΩ.65.TRANSCENDENT

## Overview
This report compares multiple scenario runs to identify cross-scenario trends, recurring micro-events, heuristic performance, emergent patterns, and comparative analysis across all Ω.65.TRANSCENDENT capabilities. It allows analysts to assess systemic stability, risk propagation, effectiveness of adaptive heuristics, and transcendent domain interactions.

## Report Fields (Expanded)

### Core Fields (Original)
- scenario_ids: List of scenario identifiers being compared
- comparison_timestamp: Date and time of report generation
- summary_metrics: High-level aggregated metrics across all scenarios
- anomalies_detected: Notes on significant deviations or unexpected outcomes
- insights: Observed patterns across scenarios
- recommendations: Suggested follow-up scenarios or parameter adjustments

### Summary Metrics (Expanded)
- total_micro_events: Count across all scenarios
- average_event_impact: Mean impact rating
- heuristic_success_rate: Percentage of successful heuristic applications
- cross_domain_propagation_rate: Original domain propagation metric

**New Summary Metrics:**
- quantum_event_count: Total quantum micro-events
- consciousness_event_count: Total consciousness micro-events
- multiverse_event_count: Total multiverse micro-events
- agent_event_count: Total agent-related events
- aris_improvement_rate_mean: Average ARIS improvement across scenarios
- cross_transcendent_propagation_rate: Propagation between new domains

### Domain Aggregates (Expanded)

**Original Domains:**
- physics: aggregated stability metrics
- biology: aggregated health and outbreak metrics
- society: aggregated trust, compliance, and cohesion metrics
- technology: aggregated integrity and disruption metrics
- emergent: aggregated cascading or non-linear effects

**New Ω.65 Domains:**
- quantum: aggregated metrics across scenarios
  - mean_coherence: Average qubit coherence time
  - mean_error_rate: Average gate error rate
  - algorithm_success_rate_mean: Success rate across algorithms
  - hardware_stability_trend: Stability across runs
  
- consciousness: aggregated metrics across scenarios
  - iit_phi_mean: Average Φ value
  - iit_phi_variance: Variance in Φ across scenarios
  - gwt_workspace_activity_mean: Average workspace occupancy
  - qualia_state_distribution: Distribution of qualia experienced
  - altered_state_frequency: How often altered states occurred
  
- multiverse: aggregated metrics across scenarios
  - mean_branch_count: Average number of timeline branches
  - timeline_divergence_rate_mean: Average divergence per decision
  - universe_compatibility_range: Min/max compatibility scores
  - counterfactual_exploration_depth_mean: Average depth explored
  
- laboratory: aggregated metrics across scenarios
  - experiment_success_rate_mean: Average success rate
  - cross_lab_success_rate: Success rate of integrated experiments
  - validation_rate: Percentage of experiments validated
  
- agent: aggregated metrics across scenarios
  - mean_active_agents: Average agents involved
  - collective_efficiency_mean: Average coordination efficiency
  - communication_density_mean: Average communication frequency
  - emergent_behavior_count: Number of novel behaviors observed
  
- aris: aggregated metrics across scenarios
  - mean_improvement_velocity: Average % improvement per cycle
  - heuristic_discovery_rate_mean: Average discoveries per scenario
  - optimization_coverage: Domains optimized across scenarios

### Cross-Domain Pattern Analysis (New)

- quantum_consciousness_correlations: Relationships between quantum events and consciousness states
- quantum_multiverse_correlations: Relationships between quantum measurements and branch creation
- consciousness_multiverse_correlations: Relationships between altered states and timeline navigation
- laboratory_agent_correlations: Relationships between agent activity and experiment success
- aris_all_correlations: Impact of ARIS improvement on all domains
